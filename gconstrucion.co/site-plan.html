Who we are

“Focus on smart work, built on trust.” G-Construction Co. was founded in 1980 by Javier Garcia since then, has completed projects valued at over One billion dollars. G-Construction Co., commitment to management, supervision, and quality work has helped us earn a reputation as a company that delivers a superb product on schedule and most importantly within budget. OUR MISSION G-Construction Co. Mission Statement To help, collaborate and communicate with clients from project estimate to project completion in a way that is both honest and most importantly transparent. G-Construction Co. Goals in Support of the Mission Statement Build and expand a diverse team of full-time, long-term trustworthy workers skilled in single-family and multifamily construction and remodeling services Provide clients with exact estimates to meet their budgets. Perform our jobs well that clients will always want to use us. Maintain, improve and restore the asset value of clients properties Create safe work sites for our workers, our clients and their residents At G-Construction Co., our goal is to build long-term relationships with our clients and provide a quality product. We can handle all your repair and reconstruction needs, regardless of the size of your project. Unlike others in our industry, we do not subcontract all of our work. G-Construction Co. employs skilled workers who can handle projects large and small under our direct supervision. We also maintain strong, long-term relationships with key subcontractors. This allows us the flexibility to deliver your project on time, within budget and to your complete satisfaction every time. In addition, we offer a one-year guarantee on our work.

Services

G-Construction Co. is a single and multifamily general contractor specializing in remodeling reconstruction and property renovation since 1930. From its base of operations in Houston, Texas, G-Construction Co., reaches out to all parts of the U.S. for large scale projects involving multifamily property repairs and improvements. G-Construction Co., provides turnkey services for total property renovation. From initial property inspection to detailed budget preparation and expert craftsmanship, G-Construction Co., does it all. Environmental Remediation Services FOR MULTIFAMILY PROPERTIES The G-Construction Co., team of workmen offers single-source multifamily property environmental remediation services, including Mold Remediation and Lead Abatement Services.. Our highest priority is to protect the health and safety of residents, visitors and workers. Mold Remediation G-Construction Co., is licensed by the State of Texas as a Mold Remediation Company and Mold Remediation Contractor. If you have issues with mold damage in your property, contact us and we will help you with the entire process, even working with your insurance company if you have mold coverage.
